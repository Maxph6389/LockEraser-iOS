## 📱 How to unlock your device

Follow these steps to remove a passcode or screen lock:

1. Open the LockEraser icon on your desktop.
2. Connect your iPhone or iPad to your PC with the USB cable.
3. Select the Unlock Screen Passcode mode from the main menu.
4. Confirm your device information shown on the screen.
5. Click Start to download the matching firmware package for your device.
6. Wait for the download to finish. This size varies by model.
7. Click Unlock to begin the reset process.
8. Keep the device connected until the software displays a success message.
9. Your device will restart like a new phone.

## 🔍 Troubleshooting common issues

If you encounter problems during the process, check these common fixes:

*   Computer does not see the device: Unplug the USB cable and plug it into a different port. Use a high-quality cable from Apple to ensure a stable connection.
*   Firmware download fails: Check your internet connection. A slow connection may time out during a large download. Restart your computer and try again.
*   iTunes error: Ensure you have the latest version of iTunes installed. iTunes provides the drivers necessary for your computer to talk to your Apple device.
*   Software hangs during reset: Do not disconnect your device if the process looks stuck. Windows may take time to process the firmware. Wait at least 30 minutes before deciding the application is not responding.
